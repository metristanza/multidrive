Common Questions

How do I add a Google account?

Click the "+ Add" button in the extension popup and sign in with your Google account. Make sure to check the "Google Drive" checkbox on the permissions screen.

Why do I see an "unverified app" warning?

Our app is currently pending Google's verification review. You can safely proceed by clicking "Advanced" and then "Go to Multi-Drive Smart Copy".

How do I upgrade to the full version?

Click "Upgrade" in the extension footer, copy your Install ID, and email it to us with your $20 payment. We'll send you a license key within 24 hours.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data stays local in your browser. Files are copied directly through Google's servers - we never see or store your files.
